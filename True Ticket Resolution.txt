            <div class="bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-blue-500 p-6 rounded-r-lg shadow-sm">
                <h2 class="text-2xl font-bold text-blue-900 mb-2">The Core Proposal</h2>
                <p class="text-blue-800 text-lg leading-relaxed">
                    This project re-engineers our ticket-closing process. It shifts our operations from an "agent-centric" model (where we assume a ticket is solved) to a "customer-centric" model (where we ask the customer for confirmation). This is achieved through a simple, low-resource automation, replacing the need for expensive AI intent analysis.
                </p>

        <!-- Strategic Canvas Section -->
        <section class="my-16">
            <h2 class="text-3xl font-bold text-gray-900 mb-10 text-center">
                The Strategic Canvas
            </h2>
            <div class="grid grid-cols-1 lg:grid-cols-2 gap-8">
                
                <!-- Card 1: False Resolutions -->
                <div class="bg-white p-6 rounded-lg shadow-lg border border-gray-200">
                    <h3 class="text-lg font-semibold text-red-600 mb-2">Problem: "False Resolutions"</h3>
                    <p class="mb-4 text-gray-700">We confuse silence with satisfaction. Agents close tickets that aren't truly solved, leading to frustrated customers who give up.</p>
                    
                    <h3 class="text-lg font-semibold text-green-700 mb-2">Proposed Solution</h3>
                    <p class="mb-4 text-gray-700">A new ticket status: "Pending (Awaiting Confirmation)". This triggers an automated, interactive email to the customer.</p>
                    
                    <h3 class="text-lg font-semibold text-indigo-700 mb-2">Key Success Metric</h3>
                    <p class="text-gray-700"><strong>1. Confirmed Resolution %:</strong> (Clicks on "Yes") - Our new "North Star" metric.</p>
                </div>

                <!-- Card 2: Polluted Metrics -->
                <div class="bg-white p-6 rounded-lg shadow-lg border border-gray-200">
                    <h3 class="text-lg font-semibold text-red-600 mb-2">Problem: Polluted Metrics</h3>
                    <p class="mb-4 text-gray-700">Our "Ticket Re-open Rate" is useless. It's triggered by "Thank You" replies, which punishes good agents and hides real problems.</p>
                    
                    <h3 class="text-lg font-semibold text-green-700 mb-2">Proposed Solution</h3>
                    <p class="mb-4 text-gray-700">The new email provides two clear buttons: [ Yes, it's resolved ] and [ No, I still need help ]. This cleanly separates gratitude from failure.</p>
                    
                    <h3 class="text-lg font-semibold text-indigo-700 mb-2">Key Success Metric</h3>
                    <p class="text-gray-700"><strong>2. True Re-open Rate %:</strong> (Clicks on "No") - This now becomes a 100% accurate and actionable metric for coaching.</p>
                </div>

                <!-- Card 3: High Customer Effort -->
                <div class="bg-white p-6 rounded-lg shadow-lg border border-gray-200">
                    <h3 class="text-lg font-semibold text-red-600 mb-2">Problem: High Customer Effort</h3>
                    <p class="mb-4 text-gray-700">Customers with unsolved problems have to start all over again, creating a new ticket and re-explaining their issue.</p>
                    
                    <h3 class="text-lg font-semibold text-green-700 mb-2">Proposed Solution</h3>
                    <p class="mb-4 text-gray-700">The "No" path automatically re-opens the original ticket, adds their new details, and routes it back to the agent for immediate follow-up.</p>
                    
                    <h3 class="text-lg font-semibold text-indigo-700 mb-2">Key Success Metric</h3>
                    <p class="text-gray-700"><strong>3. Customer Effort Score (CES):</strong> (Asked after a "Yes" click) - We expect this to improve significantly.</p>
                </div>

                <!-- Card 4: Lack of Data -->
                <div class="bg-white p-6 rounded-lg shadow-lg border border-gray-200">
                    <h3 class="text-lg font-semibold text-red-600 mb-2">Problem: Lack of Data</h3>
                    <p class="mb-4 text-gray-700">We don't know the real percentage of problems we solve on the first try.</p>
                    
                    <h3 class="text-lg font-semibold text-green-700 mb-2">Proposed Solution</h3>
                    <p class="mb-4 text-gray-700">This system provides explicit "Yes," "No," and "Assumed" (no reply) data for every single ticket, giving us a true FCR baseline.</p>
                    
                    <h3 class="text-lg font-semibold text-indigo-700 mb-2">Key Success Metric</h3>
                    <p class="text-gray-700"><strong>4. Assumed Resolution %:</strong> (Tickets that auto-close after 48h) - A metric to watch and minimize.</p>
                </div>

            </div>
